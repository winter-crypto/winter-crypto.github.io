Cryptocurrencies supported

These are the cryptocurrencies Winter supports buying & selling today. We're constantly adding new coins. Email us at hello@usewinter.com if there's a coin that you want to see added!

  • Ethereum (Ticker: ETH)
  • Solana (Ticker: SOL)
  • Dogecoin (Ticker: DOGE)
  • Shiba Inu (Ticker: SHIB)
  • USDC (Ticker: USDC)

Our roadmap will be prioritized based on the top coins by marketcap via Coinmarketcap.com
