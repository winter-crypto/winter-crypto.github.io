Stock investing and crypto trading

Are you trying to integrate crypto trading alongside stock investing? If so, this end user guide is for you. This guide helps you understand a sample journey your end user will see.

ğŸš¨Note: Winter's APIs are still in beta and under active development. We encourage you to send feedback to michael@usewinter.com

What your end user will see#

  1. User signs up/opts into crypto trading

  2. KYC check

    • End user should have previously submitted KYC info. Initiate â€œinvisibleâ€ KYC (transfer customer info + documents to us in the background)

  3. Fund crypto trading account

    • Note that the crypto cash account will be a separate cash account than the stock brokerage cash account
    • Options:
      • Pre-fund a Winter business cash account and then instantly transfer cash to end user's Winter account
      • Directly fund Winter cash account from consumers bank account via Plaid (takes 2-3 business days)

  4. Quote crypto prices

  5. Trade crypto

    • Buy/sell market & limit orders
    • Fulfill best price between exchanges

  6. View assets and P&L

    • Show all crypto assets & amount
    • Show cost basis, 24h P&L, and lifetime P&L

  7. View history of trades

    • Show all trades that a user has submitted, including cancelled and unfulfilled orders

  8. Withdraw USD to bank account or crypto to a wallet address

  9. Full yearly ledger for taxes

    • Note: Winter does not offer 1099-Bs
